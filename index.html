The basic idea

Diamond wants to know reliable contractors before the next project needs them.

This page helps Diamond understand who you are, what work your company performs, and where you normally work. If there is a match, Diamond may follow up and share available work through Diamond Connect.

Who is Diamond Solutions? A commercial parking lot maintenance company managing work across multiple markets.
Why are we asking? We are building relationships with contractors before the next project needs a reliable local partner.
What is Diamond Connect? Diamond Connect is the partner portal approved contractors may use to review available work, including scope, timing, notes, photos, and pricing.
What happens if there is a fit? Diamond may follow up, review next steps, and share matching opportunities through Diamond Connect.
Not leads. Not endless bid chasing. Diamond Connect is meant to make available Diamond-managed work easier to review.

Good fit

A good Diamond contractor relationship usually starts with the basics.

Diamond is not looking for every contractor in every market. We are looking for reliable companies that can take clear commercial scopes, communicate well, and perform the work when the opportunity fits.

01
Commercial work experience Parking lots, commercial sites, access planning, traffic flow, tenants, schedules, and finished presentation.
02
Clear communication Responsive updates before, during, and after the job help keep projects moving and customers informed.
03
Work that fits your company The best opportunities are the ones that match the services, market, timing, and scope your company can handle well.
04
Direct performance Diamond is primarily looking for contractors who can perform accepted work with their own company crews and equipment.
